The Art of Design in Digital Era

Ditulis oleh Adeysta Salsabilla | 8 Mei 2025

Design is more than just visual appearance—it's the way we experience and interact with the world around us. In today’s digital era, design has evolved from traditional print and product design into a dynamic field that spans web, UX/UI, motion, and interaction.

With the rise of digital platforms, designers are expected to create not only beautiful visuals but also intuitive, accessible, and responsive user experiences. Colors like soft purples, pastel pinks, and baby blues are increasingly used to convey elegance, calmness, and creativity in personal portfolio websites.

Good design tells a story, guides a user, and evokes emotion. In a world dominated by screens, creating design that resonates on a personal level is both an art and a science. Whether you're crafting a logo, building a layout, or animating an interface, the goal remains the same: to connect with people.

In conclusion, design is an evolving conversation between creators and users. The more empathetic and thoughtful the design, the more powerful its impact becomes.
